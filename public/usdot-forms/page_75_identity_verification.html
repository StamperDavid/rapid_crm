IDENTITY VERIFICATION

To strengthen the security of your information within our systems, the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) has implemented a new identity verification process. This is designed to protect your data and ensure secure access to your company information.


Action Required: To continue with your application, you must complete the identity verification process.

Please Note: If you have already completed identity verification using your Login.gov credentials to register an application in URS, you are not required to complete verification again. Please click the 'Click Here to Proceed' button below to continue your current application or another application.

Please follow these steps:


1. Scan the QR Code on Screen
Use your mobile device's camera to scan the QR code displayed on your screen.

2. Follow the On-Screen Instructions:
Once scanned, follow the prompts to securely verify your identity.
OR
Visit an Enrollment Center to complete the identity verification process.

Click Here to view a complete list of Enrollment Centers.

3. Confirm Verification
Once your identity has been verified, please Click Here to Proceed

If you encounter issues with the Identity Verification process, please call the Contact Center at (1-800-832-5660) for assistance.


Thank you for your cooperation in helping us maintain the highest standards of security and protection of your data.
